Privacy Policy for Pet Toxic

Last updated: January 20, 2026

Overview

Pet Toxic is a reference application designed to help pet owners identify substances that may be harmful to their pets. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

Pet Toxic does not directly collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app operates entirely offline and does not require an account, login, or internet connection to access its database of toxic substances.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your saved/bookmarked articles
  • Your recent search history
  • Your display preferences (such as light/dark mode)
  • Your purchase status (ad-free upgrade)

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party. You can clear this data at any time by deleting the app.

Advertising

The free version of Pet Toxic displays advertisements provided by third-party ad networks. These services may collect certain information automatically, including:

  • Device identifiers
  • General location (city/region level)
  • App usage and ad interaction data

This information is used to serve relevant advertisements and is governed by the ad provider's privacy policy. We do not have access to or control over the data collected by these third-party services.

Ad-Free Option: Users may purchase an ad-free upgrade through the App Store. Once purchased, no advertisements are displayed and no ad-related data collection occurs.

In-App Purchases

Pet Toxic offers an optional in-app purchase to remove advertisements. These transactions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or have access to your payment information.

External Links

The app provides phone numbers for poison control hotlines. When you tap these numbers, your device's phone app will open. These calls are handled entirely by your device and carrier.

Children's Privacy

Pet Toxic does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The third-party ad networks used in the free version may have their own policies regarding children's data.

Contact Us

support@pettoxic.com

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
